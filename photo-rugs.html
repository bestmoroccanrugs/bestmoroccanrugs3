Caring for Your Photo Rugs

Proper care for photo rugs enhances their longevity and maintains their vibrant appearance. Regular vacuuming helps remove dust and debris while preserving the intricate designs crafted from multiple photos. For rugs used in indoor spaces or heavy traffic areas like garages, using a flat-weave technique can be beneficial, as it makes cleaning easier. Avoid placing these rugs near fireplaces to prevent heat damage to the velvet fibers or garment seams. If you choose a rug with optional cotton fringe, ensure it is kept clean to prevent fraying. These unique pieces of artwork can serve as stunning decorations in contemporary spaces, reflecting your personal style while adding warmth and light to any room.

  • Regularly vacuum to keep dust and dirt at bay.
  • Spot clean any stains immediately with a mild detergent.
  • Rotate the rug periodically to ensure even wear.
  • Use a rug pad underneath to prevent slipping and provide cushioning.
  • Avoid direct sunlight exposure to prevent fading of colors.
  • Follow the manufacturer's care instructions for specific cleaning methods.
  • Store properly by rolling the rug instead of folding it to prevent creases.
